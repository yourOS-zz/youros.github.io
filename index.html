youros.github.io

"yourOS is yours. Have it your way".

Welcome to yourOS, a linux distro based on Gentoo.

yourOS is meant to be clean, simple, and very customizable.

yourOS is yours meaning you get to know everything that is happening on your computer and not have to worry about spyware or big corporations spying on you.

yourOS is also meant to be lightweight, meaning it can run on older computers as well (minimum specs TBA).
